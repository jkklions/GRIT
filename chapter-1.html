CHAPTER 1

Impact

At 04:19 UTC, the sky split into precise geometric fractures. The Harvester fleet didn’t roar in; it simply appeared, as if it had always been there. Every living mind received the same command in one pulse: surrender. Cities froze. Convoys stopped. Entire militaries became statues.

Grit didn’t understand the language, but he understood pressure. He felt the fear wave hit, shook his shoulders once, and kept walking. Drones tagged him as irrelevant fauna. Twelve seconds later, six drones were shredded and one broadcast tower was down.
