CHAPTER 3

The First Anomaly

The Harvesters had plans for fleets, missiles, and insurgencies. They had no plan for one impossible animal ignoring every prediction. As Grit crossed red dust, command software began revising the definition of threat from “organized force” to “singular will.”

In orbit, an intelligence that never paused hesitated for 0.8 seconds. That was all survivors needed to transmit coordinates, all Grit needed to close distance, and all history needed to call this chapter the first anomaly.
